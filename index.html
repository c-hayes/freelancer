P & H Construction, Inc. began business in 1978 as an oilfield construction and trucking company.

Over the years, P & H Construction, Inc. has been involved in many aspects of the oil and natural gas industry including pad building, roustabout services, oilfield remediation and pipeline maintenance.

P & H Construction, Inc. has strived over the years to maintain a high level of safety and training for its employees.

Safety

Health and safety of company employees and the public is of primary importance. Applicants for employment are subject to a pre-employment drug screening, a criminal background check, and a motor vehicle report before joining the workforce. P & H Construction, Inc. holds monthly safety meetings and daily tailgate meetings as a continued effort to ensure a safe workplace.

Drug Free

P & H Construction, Inc. maintains a strict drug and alcohol testing program. The Company’s drug and alcohol testing program complies with the U.S. Department of Transportation (DOT) Regulations (49 CFR Parts 40 and 199) and is approved by National Compliance Management Services (NCMS).

Operator

P & H Construction’s OQ Program is designed to ensure that all individuals working on DOT-regulated pipeline facilities are OQ-qualified to perform specific covered tasks, to document that qualification, and to reduce the probability and consequences of incidents and accidents. All P & H Construction employees will be OQ-qualified under this program before they perform any covered tasks.

P & H Construction’s Operator Qualification (OQ) Program was developed to comply with the Office of Pipeline Safety of the U. S. Department of Transportation's (DOT) Qualification of Pipeline Personnel Regulation (49 CFR Part 192 Subpart N and Part 195 Subpart G.)
